What is FIRST?

FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) is a nonprofit organization whose ultimate goal is to expose younger students to the wonder of STEM. Through the FIRST program, students develop skills such as teamwork, leadership, and innovation in 3 different robotics programs, FLL (FIRST Lego League), FTC (FIRST Tech Challenge), and FRC (FIRST Robotics Competition). To learn more, visit https://firstinspires.org/.
